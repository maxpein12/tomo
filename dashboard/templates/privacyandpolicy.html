Tomodachi App Data Privacy Policy

Welcome to Tomodachi!

Your privacy is important to us. This Data Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Tomodachi app ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information: 1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender, and profile details.
  • Verification Information: ID documents and selfies for identity verification. Users are required to provide a selfie to ensure their ID matches their identity.
  • Payment Information: Details necessary for in-app purchases, such as payment card information, processed securely by third-party payment processors.

1.2 Usage Information

App Activity: Posts, messages, like, follow and interactions within the app.

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage statistics.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we may collect your geolocation to recommend nearby meetups and venues.

1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your app experience, monitor usage, and provide personalized content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To verify your identity and ensure the safety of the community.
  • To recommend friends, meetups, and venues based on your interests and location.
  • To process in-app purchases and send physical and virtual gifts.
  • To improve the App’s functionality and user experience.
  • To send you notifications, updates, and promotional content (with your consent).
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Public profile information such as your name, photo, interests, and status will be visible to other users.
  • Users have the right to choose if they want to accept physical gifts from other users. If a user opts to accept a physical gift, their address will remain private. Tomodachi will facilitate the delivery by sending the gift to the recipient without disclosing their address to the sender.

3.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • For payment processing, ID verification, analytics, and customer support.

3.3 Legal and Compliance Requirements

  • If required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the App.

4. Your Choices and Rights

4.1 Managing Your Information

  • Update or edit your profile information directly in the App.
  • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking “unsubscribe” in the email.

4.2 Deleting Your Account

  • You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed from our systems, except where retention is required for legal or compliance purposes.

4.3 Access and Correction

  • Request access to or correction of your data by contacting us at
    atechitsolutionsph@gmail.com

5. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive information.
  • Secure storage of verification documents.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure. Use the App responsibly and report any suspicious activity.

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed for legitimate business purposes, such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, or enforcing agreements.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties. Review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Tomodachi is strictly for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If we discover an underage user, their account will be terminated, and their data will be deleted.
